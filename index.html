The BlockChannel Show is a podcast dedicated to educating the world on the power of blockchain-based technologies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and ZCash. The show is hosted by Steven McKie, Demetrick Ferguson, and Dr. Corey Petty.

Episode 3: "Zero Knowledge" and the Future of Privacy



On this week's episode of The BlockChannel Show, hosts McKie and Dee interview the creator of ZCash, and cryptography legend, Zooko Wilcox. Dr. Corey Petty, host of The Bitcoin Podcast, joins us as a guest host as we dive into use cases for zero-knowledge proofs and encrypted messaging. Zooko even shares tips for “young, hip, and fresh developers” to join the ZCash developer community. This episode is a must-listen for those who want to know more about ZCash.
